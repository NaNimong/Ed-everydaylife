Exhibition Proposal: K-POP Demon Hunters World Tour in Edmonton

This proposal outlines the strategic plan to host the "K-POP Demon Hunters" immersive exhibition and an exclusive merchandise pop-up in Edmonton, Alberta. Based on the 2025 Sony Pictures Animation film, this event will bring the world of Rumi, Mira, and Zoey to life for fans across Western Canada.

1. Event Overview

The "K-POP Demon Hunters" exhibition is a multi-sensory journey that blends the high-energy world of K-POP with the supernatural action seen in the Netflix hit movie.

Category Details
Event Name K-POP Demon Hunters: The Edmonton Awakening
Proposed Venue Edmonton EXPO Centre (Hall A/B)
Theme Based on the 2025 Sony Pictures Animation / Netflix Film
Target Audience Gen Z & Millennials, K-POP fans, Cosplayers, Gamers
Duration 10 Days (Including 2 VIP Preview Days)

2. Immersive Exhibition Concept

The exhibition space will feature original assets and designs from the movie, creating an atmospheric experience that mirrors the film's "Tech-Noir" and supernatural aesthetic.
